ABOUT ME

Software Engineer | Musician

Graduate of University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Music and an Associate of Arts Degree in Music. For my music side, I have been playing guitar since my freshmen year of highschool (2012). From there my interesting in different instruments expanded to bass, drums, piano, and eventually ended with classical guitar in university. I have been a part of many bands and performed in numerous live shows. Most notably opening for bands such as Alien Ant Farm, Mushroomhead, HELLYEAH, and Clutch. After high school, I moved my focus away from playing in bands and more into creating my own music as well as studying the classial guitar. You can find links to my music on this website.

After graduating from Frederick Community College, I transferred to University of Maryland, Baltimore County with the intent of majoring in Music Performance. However, I moved away from that major and moved towards Computer Science. From my time as a Computer Science major at UMBC, I have been exposed to a wide range of different topics in Computer Science. I have experience in Object-Oriented Programming, Information Retrieval, Computer Architecture, Computer Security, Data Structures, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Networks, Kali Linux, Ghidra, and IDA Pro. My languages include Python, C++, C, Assembly (Intel x86), HTML, and CSS. I am now currently a Software Engineer Associate at Fannie Mae.
